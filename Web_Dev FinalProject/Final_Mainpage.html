Come visit and visit the scenic high, open, and totally not-haunted plateu of Yashima, Japan!

Famous location of a major battle of the Genpei war, history buffs will enjoy this town's rich heritage and we can guarantee that guests won't be terrorized by the rising dead of a long ago war.

At the top of Yashima is Yashima temple, one of eighty-eight temples on the Shikoku pilgrimage. Come celebrate the new year with the completely not crazy and possessed priests.

Come and ride on the famous not-debilitated cable cars and enjoy the view.

Local fauna includes the majestic and super non-deadly Oo-suzumebachi, whose stings really don't hurt like being stabbed with tiny awful knives, honest!

So, come and stay a while at quiet, scenic, not-evil Yashima, Japan. In fact, you could even stay...forever.
